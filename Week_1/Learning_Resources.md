ROS 2, the successor to ROS 1, is an open-source framework that provides tools, libraries, and conventions to simplify creating complex and robust robot behaviors across various platforms. Built from the ground up to address ROS 1's limitations, ROS 2 offers enhanced security, real-time capabilities, and broader platform support—including Windows and macOS.
